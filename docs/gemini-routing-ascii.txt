Manufacturing Order -> Routing Steps -> Work Orders (ASCII)

ManufacturingOrder[MO-12345]
|
+-- RoutingStep[RS-01]  : Cutting
|   +-- WorkOrder[WO-0001]
|   +-- WorkOrder[WO-0002]
|
+-- RoutingStep[RS-02]  : Machining
|   +-- WorkOrder[WO-0003]
|   +-- WorkOrder[WO-0004]
|
+-- RoutingStep[RS-03]  : Assembly
|   +-- WorkOrder[WO-0005]
|   +-- WorkOrder[WO-0006]
|
+-- RoutingStep[RS-04]  : Quality
    +-- WorkOrder[WO-0007]
    +-- WorkOrder[WO-0008]

Notes:
- One Manufacturing Order can generate one or more RoutingSteps depending on the routing planner.
- Each RoutingStep corresponds to a Work Order scheduled on a specific Work Center.
- Dependencies: RS-02 (Machining) often depends on completion of RS-01 (Cutting) for the same MO.
